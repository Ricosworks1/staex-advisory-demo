# Profile 5 — Balanced

**Risk level:** Moderate  
**Option analogy:** Covered call structure — participates in upside, partially protected on downside  
**Target client:** UHNWI seeking meaningful crypto exposure with active management and downside awareness

## Allocation

| Asset class | Target % | Notes |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| Bitcoin | 60% | Core anchor — religion position |
| Active thesis play (1 position) | 25% | Highest-conviction BPF thesis only |
| Stablecoins | 15% | Earning yield (Aave / protocol lending) |
| ETH | 0% | Not held in this profile |

## Risk parameters

- Max single position: 25% (Bitcoin counts toward this in rebalancing context)
- Drawdown trigger: -20% → alert, -30% → mandatory CIO review, -40% → principal escalation
- Stablecoin floor: 15% — must be maintained
- Rebalancing: Bi-weekly
- Active thesis: Only one thesis position active at a time in this profile

## Expected behavior

In a bull market: modest outperformance vs. BTC buy-and-hold due to the thesis kicker. In a bear market: meaningful outperformance vs. BTC due to stablecoin floor. Target: better risk-adjusted return than BTC over a 12-month period.

## Suitability

The default profile for a Bitcoin Suisse Bermuda UHNWI client who has meaningful but not dominant crypto exposure. Well-suited to clients who have understood the HYPE backtest and want managed exposure to thesis plays without max-aggressive risk.
