# Profile 10 — No Directional

**Risk level:** Maximum conservative  
**Option analogy:** T-bill equivalent in crypto — pure yield, no price exposure  
**Target client:** Client who wants crypto-native yield without directional risk, or who is in capital preservation mode

## Allocation

| Asset class | Target % | Notes |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| USDC / USDT | 100% | Diversified across 2–3 issuers |
| Bitcoin | 0% | |
| Any crypto asset | 0% | |

## Yield strategy

Stablecoin allocation earns yield through:
- Aave lending (primary — highest liquidity, most audited)
- Curve stable pools (secondary — additional basis points)
- Protocol diversification: never >60% in single protocol

Target yield: 4–8% APY (market-dependent). Principal does not benefit from crypto upside.

## Risk parameters

- No drawdown risk from price (stablecoins only)
- Smart contract risk: mitigated via protocol diversification and Aave's audit track record
- Stablecoin de-peg risk: USDC and USDT only — most institutionally credible issuers
- Principal review required before any change to this profile

## Expected behavior

Returns track stablecoin lending rates. No crypto upside. No crypto downside (excluding de-peg tail risk). Suitable as a temporary holding profile while a client decides on directional exposure, or as a permanent allocation for capital preservation.

## Suitability

Client explicitly declines directional crypto exposure. All moves from Profile 10 to any other profile require written client instruction and principal review.
