## The thesis (as published)

The October 2025 BPF identified Hyperliquid (HYPE) as structurally differentiated from all other L1/L2 protocols based on:

1. **Revenue model:** $13B annualized fee revenue — highest globally, generated from trading fees not token inflation
2. **Buyback mechanics:** 97% of all revenue directed to daily HYPE buybacks. $600M+ spent at the time of publication, accumulating 28.5M HYPE tokens
3. **Market position:** 70% market share in decentralized perpetual futures, $400B monthly trading volume
4. **Institutional catalyst:** Bitwise HYPE ETF filing (ticker: BHYP, 0.67% fee), December 15, 2025 — institutional access on horizon
5. **Profitability:** Unlike all other major L1s (which depend on 85–90% subsidy funding), HYPE is operationally profitable on fees alone

**Option framework classification:** ATM call on DEX dominance + institutional perps thesis. High implied volatility, strong buyback floor, clear institutional catalyst.

## The brutal test: the drawdown

HYPE did not go straight up. It fell from ~$50 (Oct 2025) to $24.83 (Jan 2026) — a **-50% drawdown** before recovering.

**What a human advisor would have done:** Cut the position at -25% (sold at ~$37). Locked in the loss. Missed the recovery to $77.

**What the AI team would have done (thesis-based holding):**
- Portfolio Manager: flagged -25% threshold, requested review
- Risk Manager: alert triggered at -30%, escalated to CIO
- Research Analyst: re-underwrote the thesis — buybacks continued, revenue unaffected, ETF filing pending
- CIO: thesis intact, structural conditions unchanged, maintained position
- Quant: signal memo — "price decline is narrative rotation, not fundamental invalidation"
- Result: position held, full recovery captured

**Lesson:** The edge of thesis-based investing is in holding through drawdowns when the thesis remains intact. The human cost (emotional selling) is the alpha. The AI team's value is removing that cost.

## Lessons for future theses

1. The buyback floor is a real fundamental signal — not just tokenomics marketing
2. Institutional ETF filings are quantifiable catalysts — time the thesis to precede them
3. -50% drawdowns happen even on correct theses — thesis re-underwriting is the discipline that captures the recovery
4. In a -47% overall market, finding the +40% asset is the entire advisory value proposition
5. Profile 10 (stablecoins) would have preserved all capital and earned yield — conservative profiles have strong relative performance in bear markets
